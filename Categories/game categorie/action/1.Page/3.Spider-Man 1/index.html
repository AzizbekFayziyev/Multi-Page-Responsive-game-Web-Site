The Amazing Spider-Man 2 is an Action-Adventure PC game which is based on the marvel comics character Spider-Man. Morality is used in a system known as “Hero or Menace”, where players will be rewarded for stopping crimes or punished for not consistently doing so or not responding. The rating system is simple, if the player responds to crimes and successfully stop them, their hero rating will go up, at the other hand falling to do so will result in players rating to decrease.
